Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about executive protection, travel security, advance operations, and protective intelligence services.

🛫 Travel Security & Advance Operations

Do you provide international travel protection? ▼

Yes. VIPS specializes in domestic and international travel security for clients requiring protection across city, state, and international boundaries. We coordinate with law enforcement and security partners globally to ensure seamless, secure movement regardless of destination.

What is advance work and how does it benefit me? ▼

Advance work is meticulous pre-engagement planning that occurs before you arrive at any location. Our team conducts site surveys, venue assessments, route planning, and threat analysis to identify vulnerabilities before they become risks.

Benefits include: Elimination of surprises, optimized routes, secured accommodations, identified contingencies, and rapid threat response capabilities should issues arise.

How long do you typically need for advance site assessments? ▼

Advance timelines vary based on engagement complexity, destination, and threat level. For domestic travel, 1-2 weeks advance work is typical. International operations may require 2-4 weeks depending on geopolitical factors and operational complexity. We'll customize timelines to your schedule.

Can you coordinate protection during multiple-country trips? ▼

Absolutely. Multi-country protection is one of our specialties. We coordinate advance teams, local liaison networks, transportation logistics, and seamless handoffs as you move between jurisdictions. Each destination receives dedicated planning and on-ground advance assessment.

👔 Executive Protection

Can protection services be discreet and professional? ▼

Yes. Our agents are trained to operate seamlessly in corporate and professional environments. Low-profile protection maintains your professional image while ensuring unwavering security. We understand that effective protection doesn't draw attention or disrupt business operations.

How do you protect executives during public appearances? ▼

Public appearances require specific planning. We conduct advance venue assessments, identify escape routes, position protective personnel strategically, manage crowd flow, and coordinate with venue security. Our approach balances accessibility with security, ensuring you can engage with audiences while remaining protected.

Do you provide family protection as well? ▼

Yes. If your family members face related security risks, we provide extended protection services tailored to their specific needs—whether school transportation, daily routines, social activities, or travel. Family protection planning is integrated with executive protection strategy for seamless coverage.

What happens during emergencies or security incidents? ▼

We develop customized emergency protocols prior to engagement. In the event of security incidents, our trained team executes rapid response procedures, evacuates you to secure locations, coordinates with law enforcement, and provides ongoing support. 24/7 response capability ensures immediate escalation and action.

🎯 Operations & Planning

How do you assess my specific security risks? ▼

We conduct comprehensive threat assessments evaluating your profile, activities, operating environment, and geopolitical factors. Based on this analysis, we identify vulnerabilities and develop customized protection strategies aligned to your unique risk level and requirements.

How do you maintain confidentiality in all operations? ▼

Confidentiality is fundamental to everything we do. Our team operates under strict non-disclosure agreements, uses secure communications, maintains operational secrecy, and never references clients or operations publicly. Your privacy and protection are absolute priorities.

Can protection strategies be adjusted as situations change? ▼

Absolutely. We monitor evolving threats and adjust protection strategies accordingly. If your circumstances, schedule, or threat environment change, we adapt our approach to ensure continued effectiveness. Flexibility and responsiveness are core to our operational model.

Do you coordinate with law enforcement? ▼

Yes. We maintain liaison relationships with law enforcement agencies across jurisdictions. When appropriate, we coordinate with local police, federal agencies, and international partners to enhance threat awareness, facilitate communication, and ensure rapid response if incidents occur.

💼 Pricing & Engagement

How is pricing structured for executive protection services? ▼

Pricing is customized based on service type, duration, complexity, number of personnel required, and operational scope. We provide transparent proposals detailing all costs. Pricing models include hourly rates, daily engagement fees, retainer arrangements, or project-based pricing depending on your needs.

Are there minimum engagement periods? ▼

Engagement terms are flexible. We accommodate short-term assignments, one-time events, extended retainers, and ongoing protective relationships. Minimum periods are determined based on service type—some services require full-day minimums while others are event-specific.

Can I start with a one-time consultation or assessment? ▼

Yes. Many clients begin with consultations or threat assessments to understand their security requirements and available options. No long-term commitment is necessary to explore protection options. We'll work with you to develop a strategy aligned to your timeline and budget.
