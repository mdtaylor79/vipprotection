Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our security services

What services does VIPS Protection offer? +

VIPS Protection provides comprehensive security services including executive protection, travel security, personal protection, protective intelligence and risk mitigation, private investigations, and corporate security consulting. We also offer surveillance equipment and counter-surveillance services.

How do I schedule a consultation? +

You can schedule a consultation by visiting our Bookings page, clicking the "Request Consultation" button, or contacting us directly at 512-914-6433 or info@vipsprotection.com. We offer phone, video, in-person, and email consultation options.

Are consultations confidential? +

Yes, all consultations are completely confidential. We maintain strict privacy standards and never share client information with third parties. Your security information and personal details are protected under our confidentiality agreements.

Do you provide international protection services? +

Yes, VIPS Protection provides both domestic and international security services. We have coordination capabilities across jurisdictions and maintain liaison networks worldwide for seamless protection on a global scale.

What is the response time for urgent situations? +

We respond to consultation requests within 24 hours. For urgent or time-sensitive situations, we offer expedited scheduling and rapid deployment capabilities. Emergency contact: 512-914-6433.

What is your team's background and experience? +

VIPS Protection is veteran-owned and operated by professionals with military and law enforcement backgrounds. Our team brings operational discipline, proven expertise in protection, and years of security management experience.

Do you conduct threat assessments? +

Yes, we conduct comprehensive threat assessments and vulnerability analysis. Our risk mitigation services include detailed evaluation of your security situation, geopolitical monitoring, and customized protection planning.

Can you provide advance site surveys and planning? +

Absolutely. We conduct advance site surveys, venue assessments, and detailed route planning for all protected movements. This includes evaluating accommodations, identifying security protocols, and coordinating logistics.

What makes your protection services discreet? +

Our professional staff blends seamlessly into corporate and social environments. We provide low-profile protection that maintains your professional image and personal privacy while delivering unwavering security.

Is there a commitment required for services? +

No. Initial consultations are no-obligation. You'll receive professional security assessment and recommendations with no commitment. Engagement terms are customized based on your specific needs.
